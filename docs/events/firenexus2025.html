Across the Table, Across Borders.

In an industry where performance and reliability are everything, it's rare and refreshing to gather around a table (or multiple) - not just to eat, but to connect. On 24 Feb 2025, we did just that - 23 industry professionals from 7 different countries gathered in Singapore for an exclusive dinner event.

Patterson Pump Company, an American industrial stalwart known for its fire protection, HVAC and wastewater pumps. With roots tracing back over a century, Patterson's legacy is built on reliability, durability and engineering excellence. The company's name is etched into infrastucture projects around the world - a testament to its enduring strength. While proud of its legacy, Patterson is far from static - embracing cutting edge manufacturing and energy efficient systems, its pumps are designed to meet the complex demands of today's industrial landscape. Its innovations are driven by a deep understanding of the past, ensuring that modern solutions are grounded in proven principles.

Though their industries differ, InterContinental Singapore and Patterson Pump Company are united by a shared philosophy: innovation anchored in tradition. Whether it’s crafting unforgettable guest experiences or engineering systems that keep cities running, both continue to honor their roots while adapting to the future. This harmonious blend of heritage and progress is what keeps these institutions relevant — not just as symbols of excellence in their respective fields, but as benchmarks for how legacy brands can evolve without losing their soul.

Nestled in the heart of the historic Bugis precinct, InterContinental Singapore is a beacon of luxury that draws heavily from Peranakan culture and colonal architecture. From its stately facade to the intricately designed interiors, the hotel has made heritage not just an aesthetic but an experience - allowing guests to immerse themselves in the charm of Singapore's past. Yet, tradition has never held back progress. The hotel continues to reinvent luxury with state of the art amenitis, digital guest experiences, and sustainable practices. It is a place where history lives, not in the past, but as part of a forward-thinking philosophy that keeps the guest experience ever-evolving.

Here are some highlights captured throughout the event, showcasing key moments and memorable experiences

Joined by Patterson Pump’s International Sales Manager, Don Mealor, who opened the floor—not with a presentation, but with a conversation. Rather than one-directional speeches, we exchanged real-world insights about ongoing fire pump projects, market-specific challenges, and regional regulatory shifts. From understanding installation constraints in dense urban centers to navigating certification requirements across borders, the topics were as diverse as the dishes on the table.

What made the night truly valuable wasn’t just the expertise—it was the openness. Ideas flowed freely, feedback was candid, and even the most technical discussions felt like natural extensions of shared passion. Everyone brought something to the table, literally and figuratively.

As we closed the evening, one thing became clear: some of the most valuable industry insights come not in boardrooms, but around a shared meal—where trust is built, and ideas take root. We look forward to hosting more gatherings like this across the region.

Here’s to more nights like this—where like-minded people gather not just to talk shop, but to shape the future of the fire pump industry together.

Thank you to all who attended and contributed your perspectives.
You are helping shape the future of fire protection—one conversation at a time.
